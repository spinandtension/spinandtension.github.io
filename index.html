Synthetics

Synthetic Gut is the cheapest type of string you can buy. It is typically made of nylon and offers good playability for the price. Nothing special, just a good all-around string

Multifilaments

Offers playability most similar to natural gut string. They are made from hundreds of tiny fibers wound together. Softer on the arm and better for tension maintenance. However, you will sacrifice durability. If you are a big hitter, stay away.

Polyesters

This is the most varied style of string on the market. It can be formed into different edge profiles, while remaining durable. This allows for players to tailor their string specifically to their play style. This is what makes it th emost popular among tour professionals. However, you will gain no comfort here. If you know what you are doing then fitting a complementary set of polys to your racquet can really benefit your game!

Natural Gut

Will not lie to you, it is expensive. However, it remains smooth as butter through contact at hight tension, allowing fantastic control without having to sacrifice too much on the ole' joints. It also holds its tension very well, meaning restrings are fewer and further between!
